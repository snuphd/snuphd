About the Program

World-class research in an inspiring environment

A Ph.D. in Mathematics from Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence opens doors to careers in academia, finance, information technology, and research & development.

Our scholars work alongside distinguished faculty on problems spanning pure mathematics and its applications to data science, mathematical modelling, and beyond.

For Monsoon 2026, we are admitting students in Geometry, Functional Analysis, Algebra, Mathematical Modelling, Probability & Statistics, and Mathematical Data Science. Walk-in written tests are held in Delhi, Jaipur, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
