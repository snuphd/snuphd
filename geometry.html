Geometry & Topology Group

Exploring the shapes, spaces, and structures that underlie mathematics and physics.

About the Group

The Geometry & Topology group investigates the structure of smooth manifolds, vector bundles, connections, and related moduli problems in algebraic geometry. Current research spans differential geometry, algebraic topology, algebraic geometry, and their interactions with mathematical physics.

Active research areas include holomorphic bundles and their moduli, quiver bundles, maxfaces and special surface geometry, and combinatorial algebraic topology.

Research Themes

  • Differential Geometry
  • Algebraic Topology
  • Algebraic Geometry
  • Vector Bundles & Moduli
  • Quiver Bundles
  • Complex Geometry
  • Maxfaces & Special Surfaces
