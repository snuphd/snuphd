Functional Analysis Group

Investigating operator theory, matrix analysis, and the geometry of function spaces.

About the Group

The Functional Analysis group focuses on operator theory and matrix analysis, with particular interest in operator norms, subdifferential theory, approximate orthogonality in spaces of bounded linear operators, and best approximation problems.

Other active research directions include special operators on spaces of analytic functions (Hardy spaces, Bergman spaces), the geometry of Banach spaces, operator algebras, and connections between functional analysis and complex analysis.

Research Themes

  • Operator Theory
  • Matrix Analysis
  • Analytic Function Spaces
  • Banach Space Geometry
  • Approximate Orthogonality
  • Spectral Theory
  • Best Approximation
