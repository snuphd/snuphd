Algebra Group

Exploring the deep structure of groups, rings, Lie algebras, and their combinatorial and geometric manifestations.

About the Group

The Algebra group at SNU works on a broad range of problems in pure algebra and its interactions with combinatorics, graph theory, and topology. Current themes include the structural study of finite groups via subgroup counts, generating graphs of groups, algebraic graph theory, Lie algebras and superalgebras, valuation theory, and representation theory.

Members of the group regularly participate in national and international conferences and maintain active collaborations with algebraists across India and abroad.

Research Themes

  • Combinatorics
  • Algebraic Graph Theory
  • Lie Algebras & Superalgebras
