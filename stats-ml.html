About the Group

This interdisciplinary group bridges classical mathematics with modern data science. Research spans statistical inference based on copulas, neural codes and their geometric representations, optimization theory, compressive sensing, signal reconstruction, and the theoretical foundations of AI and machine learning.

Members work on both pure mathematical problems arising from data analysis and on applied problems with direct impact in engineering, biology, and finance.

Research Themes

  • Statistical Inference
  • Copula Models
  • Neural Codes
  • Optimization Theory
  • Compressive Sensing
  • RAG & AI Agents
  • Network Science
  • Mathematical Data Science
